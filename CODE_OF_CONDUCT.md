# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Nestra project a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio‑economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Positive examples of behavior:
* Welcoming and inclusive language
* Respectful feedback
* Accepting responsibility and apologizing when mistakes happen
* Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior:
* Sexualized language or imagery; unwelcome advances
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others’ private information without permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards and may take appropriate corrective action.

## Scope
Applies within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement
Report incidents to the maintainers via the security contact in `SECURITY.md` (for sensitive issues) or by emailing: security@dip-dev.example (placeholder—update before first release). Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

1. Correction
2. Warning

4. Permanent Ban
